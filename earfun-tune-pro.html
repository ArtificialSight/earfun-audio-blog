EarFun Tune Pro: Budget Headphones with Premium Features

The Tune Pro is an affordable pair of over‑ear headphones that packs features often found in more expensive models. Dual dynamic drivers (40 mm and 10 mm) with polymer diaphragms deliver a balanced sound, and hybrid active noise cancellation helps block out background noise during commutes or flights. Connectivity uses the latest Bluetooth 5.4 with multipoint support so you can switch between devices effortlessly.

Battery life is impressive: EarFun cites up to 120 hours without ANC and about 80 hours when ANC is enabled. Physical buttons allow reliable control, and the companion app provides equaliser presets and a theatre mode to enhance your movie‑watching experience.
