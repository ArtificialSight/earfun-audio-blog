EarFun UBOOM X: Portable Speaker with Powerful Sound

The UBOOM X is EarFun's robust portable Bluetooth speaker. It features a sturdy carrying handle and is rated IPX7, meaning it can be submerged in water for up to 30 minutes. Dynamic LED lights pulse along to your music for extra ambience.

Four drivers powered by 80 watts of amplification and EarFun's JumboBass technology provide balanced, room-filling sound. A long-lasting battery keeps the party going for hours, and the durable build makes it a great companion for outdoor gatherings.
