EarFun UBOOM X: Portable Speaker with Powerful Sound

The UBOOM X is EarFun’s robust portable Bluetooth speaker. It features a sturdy carrying handle and is rated IPX7, meaning it can be submerged in water for up to 30 minutes. For a bit of ambience, the speaker has dynamic LED lights that pulse along to your music.

Four drivers powered by 80 W amplification and EarFun’s JumboBass technology provide balanced, room‑filling sound. A long‑lasting battery keeps the party going for hours, and the entire design exudes durability, making it an excellent companion for outdoor gatherings.
